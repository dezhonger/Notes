## Frobenius Coin Problem (Two Variables)

Let $a$ and $b$ be **positive integers** such that $\gcd(a,b)=1$.

Consider all integers representable in the form

$$
ax + by,\quad x,y \in \mathbb{Z}_{\ge 0}.
$$

### Results

**(1) Largest non-representable integer (Frobenius number).**

$$
g(a,b)=ab-a-b.
$$

**(2) Number of non-representable integers.**

$$
N(a,b)=\frac{(a-1)(b-1)}{2}.
$$
